Climate Justice:

Welcome to the Climate Justice page!

This social issue has to do with the exploitive and unsustainable relationship humanity has with fossil fuels, driven by enviromental racism, and late-stage capitalism. With emissons increasing and natural diasters intensifying due to the runaway climate crisis, and the multitude of intersectional impacts the climate crisis has on frontline communities and Black and Brown people around the world, it's imparative to be educated on the crisis.
