Racial Equality:

Welcome to the Racial Equality page!

This social issue is concerned with the inequalities present between racial groups such as Hispanic, Asian, and more. However, the discrimination around African-Americans mostly within America has recently become an important topic due to their historical events with white people, especially in combination with criminal injustice and economic-related issues. Other minority groups have faced similar injustices as well.
