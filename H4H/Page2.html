Gender Equality:

Welcome to the Gender Equality page!

This social issue is about the differences of opportunities between the two primary genders: male and female. Throughout history, it has been debated whether females tend to have less opportunities than males, which can be seen in the 21st century today. Additionally, this has roused a call to feminism to protect women’s rights.
