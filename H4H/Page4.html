Racial Equality:

Welcome to the LGBTQ rights page!

This social issue is focused on advancing the rights of the LGBTQ+ community, which historically have been persecuted on the basis of their sexuality and gender identity. While a great deal of progress has been made in recent years, and American society is far more accepting of LGBTQ people than they were decades ago, there is still a long way to go, not just in America but across the world.
